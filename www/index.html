Georgia Overall COVID-19 Status

Below you will find information on the total number of people tested, total COVID-19 cases, negative cases, pending cases, hospitalizations and deaths attributed to COVID-19

A confirmed case is defined as a person who has tested positive (either “Confirmed” or “Presumptive Positive” for 2019 novel coronavirus. A confirmed case is defined as a case that has been laboratory confirmed by the Centers for Disease Control (CDC) and a presumptive positive case is a case that has been laboratory confirmed by a lab outside of the CDC. Healthcare providers diagnose patients with COVID-19 and they along with laboratories report the COVID-19 cases to the GA Department of Health. These numbers are preliminary and may change as more information is gathered on a person under investigation.
